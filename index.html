Jardim do Mar

Welcome to Paradise in the Pearl of the Atlantic. This house is in the center of the small parish of Jardim do Mar , known for it’s sunny and hot weather throughout the year. Jardim is the perfect place to escape big cities drama and relax , enjoy the two beaches 7 minutes walk from the house, Joe’s bar right across the corner with the best meals, amazing sunsets that will take your breath away and the hospitality of the locals to help if needed.

Levada das 25 Fontes

Its one of the most popular hiking trails among tourists and locals. About 4.3 km long and with an estimated duration of 3 hours, this is the right trail for anyone who wants to be surprised by the exuberance of Nature.

Levada do risco

Levada do Risco is a trail of easy difficulty and with an estimated duration of 2 hours. It is at an altitude of about 1000 metres, thus offering access to unobstructed views over Madeira's natural heritage.

Casa do Rabaçal

After enjoying the 25 fountains, continue towards the risk, where you can admire a splendid waterfall. Finish the route with a visit to the Casa do Rabaçal, which seems tiny compared to the imposing mountains that surround it. Take the opportunity to regain strength with a snack or simply to relax.
